About Me

Enjoying the northwest, staying close to nature, and finding joy in tech work, I moved to Seattle many years ago. Recently I have been learning more on the development side, interested in GitHub, APIs, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Node.js, and various other front-end technologies.
